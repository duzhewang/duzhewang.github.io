Duzhe Wang

I am a research scientist on the Real World and Access Analytics team in the Statistics, Data, and Analytics division at Eli Lilly and Company. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Statistics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison under the supervision of Professor Po-Ling Loh. My dissertation is on efficient statistical learning of complex data. My current research interests include statistical machine learning, causal inference, and their applications in healthcare, finance and technology industries.

Before joining UW-Madison, I received my BS in mathematics from Jilin University in 2013 and my MS in mathematics from the University of Florida in 2015.
